Overview

Being over 13 meters when standing fully upright, Brachiosaurus is far and away the largest dinosaur found in Jurassic Park. The word 'Brachiosaurus' means 'arm lizard' in latin, referring to the fact that brachiosaurus has larger forelimbs rather than larger hindlimbs. Due to this, It's torso is angled upwards quite significantly. The second part of it's name, 'Altithorax', means 'deep chest', referring to the relatively large and deep chest the Brachiosaurus had compared to most other sauropods. Because Brachiosaurus weighed around 30 tonnes, it had to eat around 300 kilogram of plants like conifers and tree ferns. This is so much that it has to eat for around 15 hours every day just to sustain itself.
